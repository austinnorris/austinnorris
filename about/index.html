About

Hi! I’m a software developer and systems administrator currently working at the UCLA Department of Earth, Planetary, and Space Sciences. I help with various satellite research missions studying space weather phenomena. I got my start working on the Electron Losses and Fields Investigation (ELFIN) mission as an undergraduate student, and now have the opportunity to mentor current student members of the project. I’ve started this blog to document some things I’ve learned or worked on, random musings on software engineering and IT, or whatever else might be interesting.
